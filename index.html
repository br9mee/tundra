About the Guild

Tundra was founded on day one of Kezan and has called it home ever since. We are a social and welcoming community built around players who want to perform well without the pressure of a hardcore environment.

Many of our members are experienced and skilled, but we prioritise good atmosphere over rigid demands. We also have an active PvP core for those who want to take the fight beyond the raid.
